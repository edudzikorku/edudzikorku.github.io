About

Core Values

Collaboration

I believe in the power of collaboration and actively engage with interdisciplinary teams, stakeholders, and experts. By working together, I leverage diverse perspectives and collective expertise to deliver comprehensive and impactful spatial-based solutions.

Innovation

I embrace innovation and constantly explore emerging technologies and methodologies in spatial data analysis. By staying at the forefront of advancements in my field, I deliver cutting-edge solutions that drive innovation and create value for my clients.

Ethics

I am committed to upholding the highest ethical standards in my work. I prioritise data privacy, security, and responsible use of spatial data. I ensure that my solutions are transparent, accountable, and aligned with ethical principles to build trust with clients and stakeholders.

Education Background

My academic journey began at the University of Cape Coast, with a focus on Geography and Regional Planning. Over four years I deepened my understanding of development planning, urban and rural management, and disaster planning.

Exposure to GIS and Remote Sensing opened a world of possibilities. I led a student team researching hazards posed to nearby settlements by LPG stations in the Cape Coast Metropolis. This project reinforced my commitment to applying spatial knowledge for societal benefit.

Self-Learning Journey

My journey into programming began when I realised it could streamline spatial data tasks. Starting with Python, I invested in SQL to sharpen database query skills. To publish maps on the web, I studied HTML, JavaScript, and CSS.

This blend of academic foundation and practical self-study equips me to provide reliable spatial data services - from data collection and geocoding to cartography and interactive web mapping.
