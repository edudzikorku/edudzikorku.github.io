Data collection services are a crucial component of any research or analysis project in that they provide the foundation upon which valuable insights and conclusions are drawn. Data is often incomplete, inconsistently formatted, or otherwise inaccurate. Data cleaning (or data engineering) is a critical step in the analysis process, ensuring the data is accurate, consistent, and free from errors.
