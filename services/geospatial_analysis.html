I have extensive experience working with a wide range of spatial data, from satellite imagery and GPS tracking data to census data and business location data. I use a variety of tools and techniques, including geographic information systems (GIS), spatial regression, and spatial clustering, to help clients make sense of their data and gain deeper insights into their businesses and operations.

Here, the focus is to help clients unlock the full potential of their spatial data, providing them with the insights and information they need to make informed decisions and drive meaningful results. Whether you're looking to analyze customer behavior, track the movement of goods, or gain a deeper understanding of the spatial patterns underlying your data, I have the expertise and experience needed to help you achieve your goals.
